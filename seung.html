Pork And Beef Baby Food Recipes

Popsicle recipe that will warm your body!

Make this warm recipe for the winter and enjoy!
ingredients

  • 1 medium Sweet Potato
  • 1 pork Chop
  • Water for cooking

  1. Peel Sweet Potato
  2. put it in the bowl
  3. put 500ml water
  4. Cook it for about 2 hours

Your popsicles are ready for winter!
